Meet Our Rooms!

Summary of our Acommodations

Our boutique luxury resort boasts a collection of Junior suite rooms and cottages, each meticulously designed with the couple in mind. From opulent Old European-inspired decor to the natural beauty of Haliburton style, every accommodation promises a unique and unforgettable experience for you and your partner. Indulge in spacious accommodations featuring Jacuzzis, wood-burning fireplaces, hot tubs, and an array of thoughtful amenities designed to enhance your romantic getaway.

For over five decades, Couples Resort has been dedicated to fostering meaningful connections between couples. As a 100% Canadian-owned establishment, owned by the LeBlanc Family since 1958, we take pride in our heritage and commitment to excellence.

Experience the magic of Couples Resort and create lasting memories in the heart of Ontario's stunning wilderness.

All visits include daily 5 course dinner and breakfast
